Accept part-qualified and schedule-like UK citations when extracting CLML provisions: `schedule/N/part/M/paragraph/P`, paragraph-less parts (`schedule/N/part/M`), unnumbered outer schedules (`schedule/paragraph/P`, section absent), and internal schedules served as appendices (`appendix/N/paragraph/M`). The citation model, CLML parser, corpus path builder, and fetcher URL builder now preserve the part and appendix segments and no longer drop the paragraph when the schedule is unnumbered, so SI 2012/2885 and SI 2012/2886 council-tax-reduction applicable-amount provisions are ingestable.
