Seeded a typed concept registry: the versioned vocabulary of input/output names the Axiom stack uses, mechanically extracted from `rulespec-us`/`rulespec-uk` and published as per-jurisdiction YAML package data under `src/axiom_corpus/concepts/data/`. Each concept records its RuleSpec legal id, kind (input/output), type fields where derivable from the defining rule (never guessed), defining module(s), occurrence count, and PolicyEngine-US oracle mapping edges imported from `axiom-encode`. Ships a deterministic, re-runnable generator (`scripts/build_concept_registry.py`), a loader package (`axiom_corpus.concepts`), a snapshot-consistency test that embeds the scanned rulespec commit SHA, and `docs/concept-registry.md`. Read-only seed; binding/enforcement is out of scope (Phase A A3).
