Published the citation-path grammar as a versioned schema (A6). `schema/citation-path.v1.json` (machine-readable, empirically derived from all 49,180 provision records) and `schema/citation-path.v1.md` define segment types per jurisdiction/document_class, the allowed charset, the family conventions (US/UK/Belgian statute and regulation, `block-N`, `page-N`), and a ratcheted known-irregulars list. `scripts/validate_citation_paths.py` validates every citation path, enforces the irregular-family and identity-drift ratchets, and runs as a new CI gate; `tests/test_citation_path_grammar.py` covers the real corpus and proves the validator rejects violations. `docs/provision-identity.md` documents that provision IDs are `uuid5("axiom:" + citation_path)` (path changes are identity changes) and specifies the rename/split/merge path-mapping event vocabulary. `docs/granularity-policy-proposal.md` analyzes axiom-programs#14 and recommends section-rollup with a subpath anchor over subsection re-segmentation.
