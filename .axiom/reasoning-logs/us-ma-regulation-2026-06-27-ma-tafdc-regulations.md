US Massachusetts TAFDC regulation corpus ingest reasoning

Date: 2026-06-27

Goal

Make the official Massachusetts TAFDC regulation provisions needed for active
PolicyEngine parity available to Axiom encoders without hand-writing corpus
rows.

Source selection

- Used the official Mass.gov Department of Transitional Assistance regulations
  landing page for 106 CMR chapters 701 through 707 because those chapters
  govern Transitional Cash Assistance, including TAFDC nonfinancial eligibility,
  financial eligibility, related benefits, auxiliary activities, and employment
  services.
- Used the official Mass.gov TAFDC Table of Need and Payment Standards page for
  106 CMR 704.410 and 704.420 because the current regulation text points those
  table values to Mass.gov/TAFDCPayments.
- Chose DOCX snapshots for chapters 701 through 707 because Mass.gov publishes
  official DOCX versions and they preserve section labels more cleanly than the
  corresponding PDFs for this corpus extractor.

Method

- Added `manifests/us-ma-tafdc-regulations.yaml` with seven official Mass.gov
  DOCX regulation chapters and the official Mass.gov HTML payment-standards
  page.
- Added labeled DOCX section extraction support to the official-document
  adapter so 106 CMR section rows can be generated by the ingestion tool rather
  than written manually.
- Ran:
  `uv run --extra dev axiom-corpus-ingest extract-official-documents --base data/corpus --version 2026-06-27-ma-tafdc-regulations --manifest manifests/us-ma-tafdc-regulations.yaml`
- No provision JSONL rows, inventory rows, coverage rows, or source snapshots
  were edited by hand.

Result

- Generated 8 official source snapshots.
- Generated 217 normalized provision records, including 209 section/block
  records.
- Coverage reported `coverage_complete: true`, with 217 sources matched, 217
  provisions, zero missing rows, zero extra rows, and no duplicate citations.
