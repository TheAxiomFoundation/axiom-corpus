# Ingestion reasoning — us-md/guidance/2026-07-12-md-fia-snap-fy2026

## Why these documents

The SNAP QC administrative-data oracle is expanding to Maryland (722 in-scope
FY2024 reviews, 100% pre-flight ceiling). Encoding Maryland's SNAP standard
utility allowances in rulespec-us requires FY 2026 amounts from primary
Maryland sources; none existed in the corpus.

- **AT 26-05 (SNAP Mass Changes for October 2025)** is the Family Investment
  Administration's annual mass-change transmittal: the FFY 2026 COLA table
  and the utility allowance schedule — SUA $572, LUA $350, telephone $40,
  effective 2025-10-01 (page 4). Issued 2025-10-03; "Revision Date(s): Not
  applicable" (no mid-year revision, unlike FY 2024's January bump).
- **SNAP Manual Section 600 (Standards for Income and Deductions, revised
  August 2025)** is the codified standards section; page 1 restates the same
  three amounts, corroborating the transmittal.
- **SNAP Manual Section 214 (Utility Allowances, revised September 2025)**
  carries the eligibility rules the allowance amounts hang off (SUA
  heating/cooling entitlement, LUA two-utility requirement, telephone-only
  standard; no internet/broadband category as of this revision).

## Notes

- AT 26-05's page footers carry a stale "Updated: July 3, 2025" template
  date; the cover page controls (issued 2025-10-03, effective 2025-10-01).
- Section 600's filename says SEPT-2025 while the in-document label says
  REVISED AUGUST 2025; content matches AT 26-05 exactly.
- AT 26-08 (H.R. 1 energy-assistance treatment, effective 2025-11-01)
  changes SUA *eligibility verification* for non-elderly/disabled
  LIHEAP-only households, not the amounts; deliberately out of scope here.
