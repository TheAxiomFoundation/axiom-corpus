# Oregon 2026 Publication OR-ESTIMATE tax-rate charts (2026-07-22)

This scope preserves the Oregon Department of Revenue's official tax-year-2026
Publication OR-ESTIMATE, Oregon Estimated Income Tax Instructions. The six-page
PDF is document number 150-101-026, revised October 7, 2025.

Page 3 provides the indexed 2026 personal-income-tax schedules. Chart S applies
to single taxpayers and married taxpayers filing separately: its bracket
thresholds are $4,550, $11,400, and $125,000; its corresponding base amounts
are $216, $678, and $10,618; and its rates are 4.75, 6.75, 8.75, and 9.9
percent. Chart J applies to married taxpayers filing jointly, heads of
household, and qualifying surviving spouses: its thresholds are $9,100,
$22,800, and $250,000; its base amounts are $432, $1,357, and $21,237; and it
uses the same four rates.

Official source:
