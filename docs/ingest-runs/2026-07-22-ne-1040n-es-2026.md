# Nebraska 2026 Form 1040N-ES estimated-tax schedule (2026-07-22)

This scope preserves the Nebraska Department of Revenue's official tax-year-
2026 Form 1040N-ES booklet, *Nebraska Individual Estimated Income Tax Payment
Vouchers*. The eight-page PDF is document number 8-014-2025, revised November
2025.

Page 6 provides the 2026 Nebraska Estimated Income Tax Rate Schedule. Single
taxpayers and married taxpayers filing separately use thresholds of $4,130,
$24,760, and $39,900. Married taxpayers filing jointly and surviving spouses
use thresholds of $8,250, $49,530, and $79,800. Heads of household use
thresholds of $7,700, $39,620, and $59,160. Every schedule applies rates of
2.46, 3.51, 4.55, and 4.55 percent. Page 4 also provides 2026 standard
deductions of $8,850 for single or married filing separately, $17,700 for
married filing jointly, and $12,950 for head of household.
