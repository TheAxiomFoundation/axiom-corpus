# Kansas 2026 K-40ES income-tax schedule (2026-07-21)

This scope preserves the Kansas Department of Revenue's official Form K-40ES
for tax year 2026, revised August 26, 2025. The form publishes the operative
individual income-tax schedules after the August 15, 2025 determination window
under K.S.A. 79-32,110c: married joint filers pay 5.2 percent through $46,000
and $2,392 plus 5.58 percent above $46,000; all other individual filers pay 5.2
percent through $23,000 and $1,196 plus 5.58 percent above $23,000.

Artifacts are generated without publication or database loading:

```bash
uv run --extra dev axiom-corpus-ingest extract-official-documents \
  --base data/corpus \
  --version 2026-07-21-ks-k40es-2026 \
  --manifest manifests/us-ks-2026-k40es.yaml
```

The extraction snapshots the official PDF and normalizes its complete text as
one body-bearing child under a document root so the tax computation schedule
remains intact. The unpublished successor release selector
`us-rulespec-2026-07-21-ks-k40es-current` adds this guidance scope to
`us-rulespec-2026-07-21-pa-pit-current` without changing existing scope
membership. Publication and serving activation are separate, reviewed steps.
