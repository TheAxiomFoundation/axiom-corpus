§1433.  Publication of opinions and decisions

The board shall provide for the publication of such of its reports, opinions and decisions or judgments as are of public interest, in such form as it may deem best adapted for public convenience and use, and such authorized publication shall be competent evidence of the reports of the board for purposes of all courts of the state, without any further proof or authentication thereof.  The board's internal deliberations concerning a pending matter shall be considered judicial proceedings for the purposes of R.S. 42:17(B).

Acts 2014, No. 198, §1, eff. July 1, 2014.
