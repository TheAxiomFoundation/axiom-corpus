RS 47:322.45     

§322.45. Disposition of certain collections in Concordia Parish

            The avails of the tax imposed by R.S. 47:321 from the sales of services as defined in R.S. 47:301.3(1) in Concordia Parish under the provisions of R.S. 47:321(C) and 322, as applicable, shall be credited to the Bond Security and Redemption Fund, and after a sufficient amount is allocated from that fund to pay all of the obligations secured by the full faith and credit of the state which become due and payable within any fiscal year, the treasurer shall pay the remainder of such funds into the Concordia Parish Economic Development Fund as provided in and subject to the provisions of R.S. 47:302.53.

            Acts 2002, 1st Ex. Sess., No. 2, §1, eff. July 1, 2002; Acts 2025, No. 384, §8(B), eff. June 20, 2025.
