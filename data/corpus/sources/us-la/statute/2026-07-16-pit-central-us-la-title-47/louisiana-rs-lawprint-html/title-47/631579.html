RS 47:2204     

§2204. Additional terms of ordinance

            The ordinance allowing for the public sale of adjudicated property, sale of immovable property to enforce a tax lien certificate held by a political subdivision, or assignment of a tax lien certificate issued to a political subdivision may provide that the public sale may be subject to terms and conditions imposed by the political subdivision in the ordinance. An ordinance adopted pursuant to this Subpart may provide that a subsequent ordinance is required to approve the sale.

            Acts 2008, No. 819, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2009; Acts 2010, No. 947, §1; Acts 2024, No. 774, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2026; Acts 2025, No. 411, §1, eff. Jan. 1, 2026.
