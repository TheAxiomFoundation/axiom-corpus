RS 47:337.16.4     

§337.16.4.  Charge for collection; remitting amounts collected

In the event such agreement is entered into, pursuant to R.S. 47:337.16.1, the collector of revenue shall make no charge for the collection of such taxes.  He shall remit monthly to the treasurer of the city of New Orleans all amounts collected by him under authority of R.S. 47:337.16 through 337.16.6 not later than the tenth day of the month following their collection.

Acts 1950, 2nd Ex.Sess., No. 11, §4; Redesignated from R.S. 33:2750 pursuant to Acts 2011, No. 248, §4.
