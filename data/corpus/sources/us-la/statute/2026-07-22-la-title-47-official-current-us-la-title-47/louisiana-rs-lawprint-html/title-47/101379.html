RS 47:1901.1     

§1901.1.  Application of other laws

A.  All other pertinent provisions of the laws of the state of Louisiana shall apply to the assessor of each assessment district in the same manner and to the same extent as they apply to one assessor in each of the several parishes of the state of Louisiana.

B.  All laws pertaining to execution and cancellation of bonds by assessors shall apply to said tax assessors.

Acts 1970, No. 155, §4; Acts 2006, No. 622, §8, eff. Dec. 11, 2006.
