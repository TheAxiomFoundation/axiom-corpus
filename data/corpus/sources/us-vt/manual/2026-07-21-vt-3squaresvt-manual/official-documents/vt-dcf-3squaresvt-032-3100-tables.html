3100.1 Determining Qualifying Work Quarters (QQs)

3100.1.1 Qualifying Work Quarters Prior to 1978

  1. A qualifying work quarter was earned for each  Calendar Quarter a person was paid $50 or more in wages (including  Migrant Worker and  Seasonal Farmworker wages).
  2. Four quarters were earned for each taxable year in which self-employment earnings were $400 or more.
  3. A quarter was earned for each $100 (limited to four) of migrant or seasonal farmworker wages for years 1955-1977.

3100.1.2 Qualifying Work Quarters for 1978 and Later

  1. Based solely on total yearly earnings.
  2. Divide the non-citizen’s total earned income by the increment amount for the year. (All types of earnings)
  3. No more than four quarters can be earned in each year.
  4. The following chart shows the increment amount for each year.
