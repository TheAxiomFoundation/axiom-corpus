700 Identity

700.1 Overview

  1. Verify the identity of the person applying. 
  2. Verify the identity of an  AR applying on behalf of a household and the  Head of Household.  
  3. Identity is the only mandatory verification when providing  Expedited Service. See Expedited Service, Verification of Identity.

700.2 Verification

700.2.1 Documentation

  1. Verify identity using readily available documentary evidence, or if unavailable, through a collateral contact. Accept any documents which establish the applicant or authorized representative’s identity.
    1. Note: Numident for persons with an SSN verifies identity.
    2. See Examples of Accecptable Verification.

700.2.2 Application

  1. Deny the application if not verified by the due date.
  2. Verify identity before providing expedited service.
