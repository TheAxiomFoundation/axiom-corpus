Notices

7 CFR § 273.10(g)(1)

Approval Notices

7 CFR § 273.10(g)(1)(i)(A)-(C)


Provide a W-0001N, Notice of Action, when the household is determined eligible for benefits.  


Provide the following information in the approval notice: 

  • benefit amount,
  • benefit level changes at approval,
  • benefit period,
  • combined benefit amounts when appropriate,
  • contact name and number for questions, 
  • fair hearing rights, 
  • legal services availability,
  • other information deemed necessary, and
  • requirement to reapply at the end of the benefit period.

Denial Notices

7 CFR § 273.10(g)(1)(ii)


Provide a W-0001N, Notice of Action, when the household is determined ineligible for benefits.


Provide the following information in the denial notice: 

  • the reason for the denial, 
  • their right to a fair hearing, 
  • their right to reapply,
  • contact information for questions, and
  • the actions the household must take to reactivate the application within 30 days of the notice date when denied for not completing required actions.  

       Note:  A new application is required if: 

  • the application is not reactivated within 30 days of the notice date, or 
  • when denied as SNAP ineligible.
