Electronic Notices

7 CFR § 272.3(c)(1)-(6) & 7 CFR § 273.18(n)(2)(i)-(ii)

Waiver 2160010 of 7 CFR § 273.2(c)(5) and (e)(3) & 7 CFR § 273.10(g)(1)(i) and (ii) and (2) & 7 CFR § 273.13(a)(3) & 7 CFR § 273.14(b)(1) & 7 CFR § 273.15(l) & 7 CFR § 273.16(b)(13) and (e)(3) and (9) and (f)(1) and (3) and (g)(3) and (h) & 7 CFR § 273.18(e)(3)(i)-(vi) and (4)(i)-(ii) and(g)(2)


EDGs have the option to receive and view their notices online. EDGs must electronically sign a disclosure statement and agree to receive and read electronic notices sent by DSS. A confirmation email and paper notice are sent to the EDG with instructions on how to log into their ConneCT Account to view notices. 


Emails with a link to the EDGs home page are sent to the EDG whenever a new notice is added to their account. EDGs choosing the online option receive all notices online. 

Exception: EDGs are mailed paper notices for tax offsets of a claim overpayment. 


The EDG can opt out of electronic notices at any time. DSS will opt out the EDG from receiving electronic notices if the EDG’s email account is invalid or no longer active. The EDG can opt in again at any time by providing a valid email address.


DSS must provide paper copies of any notices upon request by the EDG.
