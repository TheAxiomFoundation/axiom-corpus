VA - Veteran’s Administration


Vehicle - Any motorized device that transports individuals or property.


Verified Upon Receipt - Verified upon receipt means the information is not questionable, and the provider is the primary source of the information. No further verification is required.


Voter Registration Coordinator (VRC) - A designated worker at DSS regional offices in charge of:

  • maintaining supplies of ED-671A/S, Mail-In Voter Registration Application Forms,
  • verifying completeness of the voter registration applications,
  • sending W-619, Receipt of Application, to individuals who submitted voter registration applications through DSS, and
  • forwarding voter registration applications to the appropriate town hall within 10 days of receipt.
