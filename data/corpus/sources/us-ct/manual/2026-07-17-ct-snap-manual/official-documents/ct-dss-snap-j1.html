J

Job Corps - 7 CFR § 273.9(b)(1)(i) and (iii) and (v) & (c)(5)(i)(A) and (8) - Count salary and performance bonuses as earned income for Job Corps participants and former participants who are still receiving Job Corps payments. 


Exclude any lump sum payment of salary from prior periods as a lump sum (nonrecurring) payment.  


Exclude as a lump sum (nonrecurring) performance bonuses paid in one payment, received after the individual leaves the program.


Exclude training allowances.


Job Related Reimbursement - 7 CFR § 273.9(c)(5) - Exclude reimbursements from the employer for job-related expenses including travel expenses and uniform allowances.


Jury Duty Payments - 7 CFR § 273.9(b)(1)(i) & (c)(2) and (5) - Count as earned income.

Exception:  Exclude as a reimbursement for things such as transportation, childcare, or lodging.


Determine the intent of the payment from the payor.
