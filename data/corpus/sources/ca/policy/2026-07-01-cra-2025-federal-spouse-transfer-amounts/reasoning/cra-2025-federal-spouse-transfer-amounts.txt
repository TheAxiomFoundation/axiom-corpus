Title: CRA Schedule 2 2025 Federal Amounts Transferred from your Spouse or Common-Law Partner.

Source:
- https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/5000-s2/5000-s2-25e.pdf

CRA Schedule 2 2025:
Line 1 transfers the spouse or common-law partner age amount from line 30100.
Line 2 transfers the Canada caregiver amount for infirm children under 18 from line 30500.
Line 3 transfers the pension income amount from line 31400, maximum 2000.
Line 4 transfers the disability amount from line 31600.
Line 5 transfers the federal tuition amount designated to the claimant.
Line 6 adds lines 1 to 5.
Line 7 uses spouse taxable income from line 26000 if it is 57375 or less. If taxable income is more than 57375, line 7 is the amount from line 76 divided by 14.5%.
Line 11 adds spouse amounts from line 30000, line 96, and line 32300.
Line 12 is line 7 minus line 11, floored at zero.
Line 13 is line 6 minus line 12, floored at zero, and is entered on line 32600.
