Active families benefit 
 
If your adjusted family income is $120,000 or less, you can claim the active 
families benefit. Otherwise, enter "0" on line 8. 
 
Notes: 
If your spouse or common-law partner died at any time during the year, do not 
include their income when you calculate the adjusted family income. 
 
If you are claiming the credit on a deceased person's final return, do not 
include the surviving spouse or common-law partner's income when you 
calculate the adjusted family income.
