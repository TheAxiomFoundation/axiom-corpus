Title: CRA Schedule 5 2025 Amounts for Spouse or Common-Law Partner and Dependants.

Source:
- https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/5000-s5/5000-s5-25e.pdf

CRA Schedule 5 2025:
Line 30300 calculates the spouse or common-law partner amount. Line 1 is the basic personal amount from line 30000. Line 2 enters 2687 if the spouse or common-law partner is eligible for the Canada caregiver amount. Line 3 adds lines 1 and 2. Line 4 is spouse or common-law partner net income from line 23600. Line 5 is line 3 minus line 4, floored at zero, and is entered on line 30300.
Line 30400 calculates the amount for an eligible dependant. Line 1 is the basic personal amount from line 30000. Line 2 enters 2687 if eligible for the Canada caregiver amount for the dependant other than an infirm child under 18. Line 3 adds lines 1 and 2. Line 4 is the dependant net income from line 23600. Line 5 is line 3 minus line 4, floored at zero, and is entered on line 30400.
Line 30425 calculates the Canada caregiver amount for spouse or common-law partner, or eligible dependant age 18 or older. Line 1 base amount is 28798. Line 2 is the person's net income from line 23600. Line 3 is line 1 minus line 2, floored at zero and capped at 8601. Line 4 is the amount claimed on line 30300 or line 30400 for the person. Line 5 is line 3 minus line 4, floored at zero, and is entered on line 30425.
Line 30450 calculates the Canada caregiver amount for other infirm dependants age 18 or older. For each dependant, line 1 base amount is 28798. Line 2 is the infirm dependant net income from line 23600. Line 3 is line 1 minus line 2, floored at zero and capped at 8601. Line 30450 is the total allowable amounts claimed for all dependants.
Line 30500 calculates the Canada caregiver amount for infirm children under 18 years of age. The amount is 2687 for each eligible child, and the result is entered on line 30500.
