Adjusted family net income

If you have a spouse or common-law partner, your adjusted net incomes are combined to get your adjusted family net income (AFNI).

Your AFNI is:

Your family net income (line 23600 of your tax return, plus line 23600 of your spouse's or common-law partner's tax return, if applicable)
−
Any Universal Child Care Benefit (UCCB) and registered disability savings plan (RDSP) income received (line 11700 and line 12500 of your or your spouse's tax return, if applicable)
+
Any UCCB and RDSP amounts repaid (line 21300 and line 23200 of your or your spouse's tax return, if applicable)
