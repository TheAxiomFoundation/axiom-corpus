Title: CRA T4127 2026 CPP, QPP, EI, and QPIP contribution rates.

Source:

T4127 Payroll Deductions Formulas, 122nd edition, effective January 1, 2026, states that the formulas determine Canada Pension Plan contributions and Employment Insurance premium deductions and are approved for purposes of the Canada Pension Plan and Employment Insurance Act.

Table 8.3 Canada Pension Plan / Quebec Pension Plan 2026 contribution rates and amounts: CPP annual maximum pensionable earnings 74600.00; basic exemption 3500.00; employee contribution rate 0.0595; maximum annual employee contribution 4230.45. QPP annual maximum pensionable earnings 74600.00; basic exemption 3500.00; employee contribution rate 0.0630; maximum annual employee contribution 4479.30.

Table 8.4 base CPP/QPP rates and amounts: CPP base employee rate 0.0495; QPP base employee rate 0.0530; CPP maximum base employee contribution 3519.45; QPP maximum base employee contribution 3768.30.

Table 8.5 first additional CPP/QPP rates and amounts: first additional employee contribution rate 0.0100; maximum annual first additional employee contribution 711.00.

Table 8.6 second additional CPP/QPP rates and amounts: additional maximum pensionable earnings 85000.00; second additional employee contribution rate 0.0400; maximum annual second additional employee contribution 416.00.

Table 8.7 Employment Insurance 2026 rates and amounts: Canada except Quebec maximum annual insurable earnings 68900.00; employee contribution rate 0.0163; employer contribution rate 0.02282; maximum annual employee premium 1123.07; maximum annual employer premium 1572.30.
