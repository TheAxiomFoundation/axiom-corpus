If you were born before January 1, 1961, and you had a spouse on December 31, 2025, you and your spouse can jointly decide to include a portion
of your retirement income in the calculation of your spouse’s income. For more information, see “Transfer of retirement income between spouses”
in the instructions for line 122 in the guide.
NOTE
If you transfer a portion of your retirement income to your spouse, you must also transfer the corresponding Québec income tax withheld from that income.

Only the person transferring retirement income must enclose Schedule Q with their return.
