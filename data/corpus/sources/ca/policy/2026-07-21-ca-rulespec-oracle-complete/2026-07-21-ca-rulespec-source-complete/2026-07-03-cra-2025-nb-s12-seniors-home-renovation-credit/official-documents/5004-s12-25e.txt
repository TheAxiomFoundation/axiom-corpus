Schedule NB(S12) 
 
New Brunswick Seniors' Home Renovation Tax Credit 
 
Complete this schedule if you have eligible home renovation expenses. 
 
For more information about these credits, go to canada.ca/nb-tax-info. 
 
Attach a copy of this schedule to your paper return. Do not include your 
receipts, but keep them in case the Canada Revenue Agency asks to see them 
later. 
 
If you need more space, attach an additional sheet of paper.
