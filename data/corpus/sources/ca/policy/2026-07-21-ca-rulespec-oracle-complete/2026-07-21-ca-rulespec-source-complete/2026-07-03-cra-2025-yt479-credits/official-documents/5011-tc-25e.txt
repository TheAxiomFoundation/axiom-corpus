Yukon Credits 
 
You can claim these refundable tax credits even if you do not have to pay any 
tax. If the total of these credits is more than the amount of taxes you have 
to pay, you may get a refund for the difference. 
 
Complete the calculations that apply to you and attach a copy of this form to 
your paper return. 
 
For more information about these credits, go to canada.ca/yt-tax-info.

Yukon fertility and surrogacy tax credit 
 
You can claim this credit if you incurred eligible medical expenses for 
fertility care or surrogacy treatment services, you were resident of Yukon at 
the end of the year, you were insured under the Yukon Health Care Insurance 
Plan, and you had a Yukon health care card on December 31, 2025. 
 
If you are claiming the credit on behalf of your spouse or common-law 
partner, or your dependant, they must also be a Yukon-insured individual and 
they must have had a Yukon health care card on December 31, 2025.
