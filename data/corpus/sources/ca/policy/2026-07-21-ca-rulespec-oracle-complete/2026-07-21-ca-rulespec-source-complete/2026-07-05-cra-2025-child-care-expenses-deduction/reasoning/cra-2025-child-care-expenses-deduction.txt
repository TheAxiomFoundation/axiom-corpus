Title: CRA Form T778 2025 Child Care Expenses Deduction.

Source:
- https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/t778/t778-25e.pdf

CRA Form T778 2025:
Part B calculates the basic limit for child care expenses. Line 1 dollar amount is 8000 dollars. It applies to eligible children born in 2019 or later for whom the disability amount cannot be claimed. Line 2 dollar amount is 11000 dollars. It applies to eligible children for whom the disability amount can be claimed. Line 3 dollar amount is 5000 dollars. It applies to eligible children born in 2009 to 2018, and children born in 2008 or earlier with a mental or physical infirmity for whom the disability amount cannot be claimed. Line 4 adds lines 1 to 3. Line 5 enters total child care expenses from line 67950. Line 6 multiplies earned income by 2/3. Line 7 is the least of line 4, line 5, and line 6. Line 8 subtracts the higher-income person's line 21400 amount, and line 9 is the allowable deduction for the lower-income person.
Part C applies to a higher-income person when the lower-income person was in a listed school, infirmity, confinement, prison, or separation situation. Line 10 is line 4 multiplied by 2.5%. Line 11 multiplies line 10 by the number of weeks for full-time school or the listed weekly situations. Line 12 multiplies line 10 by the number of part-time school months, excluding months already counted by weeks. Line 13 adds lines 11 and 12. Line 14 is the lesser of line 7 and line 13.
Part D applies when the claimant was enrolled in an educational program in 2025. Line 15 is line 4 multiplied by 2.5%. Line 16 multiplies line 15 by full-time school weeks. Line 17 multiplies line 15 by eligible months not already counted in line 16. Line 18 adds lines 16 and 17. Line 19 is line 4 minus line 9 or line 14. Line 20 is line 5 minus line 9 or line 14. Line 21 is net income excluding lines 21400 and 23500 multiplied by 2/3. Line 22, if Part C was filled out, is line 13 minus line 6. Line 23 is the least of line 18, line 19, line 20, line 21, and line 22 if it applies. Line 24 enters line 9 or line 14. Line 25 adds line 23 and line 24 and is entered on line 21400 of the T1 return.

Numeric grounding: T778 line 1 amount is 8000 dollars. T778 line 2 amount is 11000 dollars. T778 line 3 amount is 5000 dollars. T778 Part C and Part D percentage is 2.5%.
Numeric grounding: T778 two-thirds decimal value is 0.6666666667.
