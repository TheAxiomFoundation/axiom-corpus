CRA Schedule 13 2025 Employment Insurance Premiums on Self-Employment and Other Eligible Earnings

Source: https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/5000-s13/5000-s13-25e.pdf

Line 1: Total amount from line 11 plus line 27 of the return. If negative, enter 0.
Line 2: Employment income from a corporation the taxpayer controls by more than 40%.
Line 3: Tax-exempt self-employment income earned on a reserve.
Line 4: Net self-employment income, total of lines 1, 2, and 3.

Line 5: Maximum insurable earnings, $65,700.
Line 6: Total EI insurable earnings from box 24 of all T4 slips, or box 14 if box 24 is blank.
Line 7: Line 5 minus line 6.
Line 8: Enter whichever is less: line 4 or line 7.

Residents of a province or territory other than Quebec:
Line 9: Multiply line 8 by 1.64%, maximum $1,077.48, and enter the amount on lines 31217 and 42120.
If total EI premiums from boxes 18 and 55 of all T4 slips are $1,077.48 or more, line 9 is zero.

Residents of Quebec:
Line 10: Multiply line 8 by 1.31%, maximum $860.67, and enter the amount on lines 31217 and 42120.
If total EI premiums from box 18 of all T4 slips are $860.67 or more, line 10 is zero.
