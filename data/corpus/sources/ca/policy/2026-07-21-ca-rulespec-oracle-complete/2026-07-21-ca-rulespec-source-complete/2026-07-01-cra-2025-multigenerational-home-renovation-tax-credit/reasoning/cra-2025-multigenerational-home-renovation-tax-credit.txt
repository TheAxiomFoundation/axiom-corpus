Title: CRA Schedule 12 2025 Multigenerational Home Renovation Tax Credit.

Source:
- https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/5000-s12/5000-s12-25e.pdf

CRA Schedule 12 2025:
An eligible individual can claim up to 50000 in qualifying expenditures for a qualifying renovation ending in the tax year. The multigenerational home renovation tax credit is 14.5% of the lesser of 50000 and total qualifying expenditures. The maximum credit available is 7250.
Step 1 calculates qualifying expenditures. Line 1 is total qualifying expenditures. Line 2 subtracts amounts that can reasonably be considered reimbursed, including government assistance received or receivable. Line 3 is line 1 minus line 2, capped at 50000 per qualifying renovation. Line 4 is the maximum amount per qualifying renovation. Line 5 subtracts eligible expenses claimed by other eligible individuals. Line 6 is line 4 minus line 5. Line 7 is the lesser of line 3 and line 6.
Step 2 calculates the credit. Line 8 is total qualifying expenditures, adding line 7 for each qualifying renovation. Line 9 applies the 14.5% rate. Line 10 multiplies line 8 by the rate and enters the result on line 45355, subject to a maximum of 7250 per qualifying renovation.
