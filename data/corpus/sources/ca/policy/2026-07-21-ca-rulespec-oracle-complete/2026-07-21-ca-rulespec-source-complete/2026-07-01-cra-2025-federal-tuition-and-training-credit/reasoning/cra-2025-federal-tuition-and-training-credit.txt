Title: CRA Schedule 11 2025 Federal Tuition Amount and Canada Training Credit.

Source:
- https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/5000-s11/5000-s11-25e.pdf

CRA Schedule 11 2025:
Line 1 is eligible tuition fees paid to Canadian educational institutions for 2025, where fees for each institution must be more than 100.
Line 2 is the amount from line 1 multiplied by 50%.
Line 3 is the Canada training credit limit from the latest notice of assessment or reassessment.
Line 4 is the lesser of line 2 and line 3.
Line 5 is the Canada training credit amount claimed for 2025, cannot be more than line 4, and is entered on line 45350 of the return.
Line 6 is the Canadian tuition amount available for 2025, line 1 minus line 5.
Line 7 is eligible tuition fees paid to foreign educational institutions for 2025.
Line 8 is line 6 plus line 7.
Line 9 is unused federal tuition from the 2024 notice of assessment or reassessment.
Line 10 is total tuition amount available for 2025, line 8 plus line 9.
Line 11 enters line 26000 taxable income if it is 57375 or less. If line 26000 is more than 57375, line 11 is the result of line 76 of the return divided by 14.5%.
Line 12 is the amount from line 101 of the return.
Line 13 is line 11 minus line 12, floored at zero.
Line 14 is unused tuition amount claimed for 2025, the lesser of line 9 and line 13.
Line 15 is line 13 minus line 14.
Line 16 is the 2025 tuition amount, the lesser of line 8 and line 15.
Line 17 is the federal tuition amount, line 14 plus line 16, entered on line 32300 of the return.
Line 20 is total unused federal tuition amount, line 18 minus line 19.
Line 21 is the amount from line 8, maximum 5000.
Line 23 is unused federal tuition amount available to transfer, line 21 minus line 22, floored at zero.
Line 24 is federal tuition amount transferred, cannot be more than line 23, entered on line 32700.
Line 25 is unused federal tuition amount available to carry forward to a future year, line 20 minus line 24.
