Title: CRA Form T1-M 2025 Moving Expenses Deduction.

Source:
- https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/t1-m/t1-m-25e.pdf

CRA Form T1-M 2025:
The form calculates the moving expenses deduction entered on line 21900 of the T1 return. Part 2 applies the distance requirement: the new home must be at least 40 kilometres closer to the new work location or school by the shortest public route; line 3 subtracts the new-home distance from the old-home distance, and if line 3 is less than 40 kilometres the moving expenses cannot be deducted.
Part 4 calculates allowable moving expenses. Line 8 adds travel expenses from lines 5 to 7. Line 11 adds temporary living expenses from lines 9 and 10. Line 14 maximum amount is 5000 dollars. It applies to costs to maintain the old home when vacant. Line 15 adds lines 12 to 14. Line 20 adds old-home selling costs from lines 16 to 19. Line 23 adds new-home buying costs from lines 21 and 22. Line 24 adds lines 4, 8, 11, 15, 20, and 23. Line 25 subtracts any reimbursement or allowance not included in income. Line 26 is line 24 minus line 25, floored at zero. Line 28 is net eligible income from the new work location, self-employment source, or taxable student awards. Line 29 is the lesser of line 27 and line 28 and is entered on line 21900. Line 30 is line 27 minus line 28, floored at zero, and is the unused moving expenses available to carry forward.

Numeric grounding: T1-M distance requirement is 40 kilometres. T1-M line 14 cap is 5000 dollars.
