Title: CRA Schedule 9 2025 Donations and Gifts.

Source:
- https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/5000-s9/5000-s9-25e.pdf

CRA Schedule 9 2025:
Line 5 is total eligible amount of charitable donations, adding lines 1 to 4.
Line 6 amount A is net income from line 23600 multiplied by 75%.
Line 7 amount D is amount B plus amount C, multiplied by 25%.
Line 9 is total charitable donations limit, the lesser of amount A and line 8.
Line 10 is the lesser of line 5 and line 9.
Line 11 is eligible amount of ecological gifts and cultural gifts.
Line 12 is line 10 plus line 11.
Line 13 is the lesser of line 12 and 200.
Line 14 is line 12 minus line 13.
Line 15 is total ecological gifts made after February 10, 2014 and before 2016 included in line 11.
Line 16 is line 14 minus line 15, floored at zero.
Line 17 is taxable income from line 26000.
Line 18 is the income threshold, 253414.
Line 19 is line 17 minus line 18, floored at zero.
Line 20 amount F is the lesser of line 16 and line 19, multiplied by 33%.
Line 21 amount G is amount E from line 14 minus amount F, multiplied by 29%.
Line 22 amount H is line 13 multiplied by 14.5%.
Line 23 is allowable donations for 2025, adding lines 20 to 22, and is entered on line 34900 of the return.
