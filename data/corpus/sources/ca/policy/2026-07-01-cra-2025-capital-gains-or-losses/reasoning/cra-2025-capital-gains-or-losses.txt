Title: CRA Schedule 3 2025 Capital Gains or Losses.

Source:
- https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/5000-s3/5000-s3-25e.pdf

CRA Schedule 3 2025:
Part 3 reports total gains or losses on dispositions by property type. Lines 1 to 8 are total gain or loss amounts for QSBCS, QFFP, QFFP mortgage foreclosures and conditional sales repossessions, publicly traded shares and similar property, real estate/depreciable/other property, bonds and similar property, crypto-assets, and other mortgage foreclosures and conditional sales repossessions. Line 9 is personal-use property total gain only. Line 10 is listed personal property net gain only after subtracting unapplied LPP losses from other years. Line 11 adds lines 1 to 10.
Part 4 calculates total capital gains or losses. Line 14 is line 12 minus line 13 capital gains deferral. Line 17 adds line 14 and capital gains or losses from T5, T5013, T4PS, and T3 slips. Line 19 is line 17 minus line 18 capital loss from a reduction in business investment loss. Line 21 adds reserves from Form T2017 to line 19.
Part 5 calculates total taxable capital gains or net capital losses. Line 23 inclusion rate is 50%, equivalent to 0.50 or 0.5. Line 24 multiplies line 22 by 50%. Line 25 includes capital gains from certain partnership interests subject to 100% inclusion, floored at zero. Line 26 adds lines 24 and 25. If positive, the result is entered on line 12700. If negative, it is a net capital loss and is not reported on line 12700.
Decimal inclusion rate: 0.5
