CRA current year tax rates and income brackets (2026):
For income earned in 2026, applied to the 2026 tax return.
Federal rate for 2026:
Each portion of taxable income from $0 up to $58,523 is taxed at 14%.
Each portion from $58,523.01 up to $117,045 is taxed at 20.5%.
Each portion from $117,045.01 up to $181,440 is taxed at 26%.
Each portion from $181,440.01 up to $258,482 is taxed at 29%.
Each portion from $258,482.01 and above is taxed at 33%.

CRA T4127 Payroll Deductions Formulas, 122nd edition, effective January 1, 2026:
The formulas in the guide determine federal, provincial except Quebec, and territorial income taxes, CPP contributions, and EI premium deductions, and are approved for purposes of the Income Tax Act, the Canada Pension Plan, and the Employment Insurance Act.

Step 1: annual taxable income A is the annual taxable income amount. If the result is negative, T equals L.

Step 2: annual basic federal tax T3 equals (R x A) - K - K1 - K2 - K3 - K4. If the result is negative, T3 equals $0. For employees in Quebec, use K2Q in place of K2. R and K are based on 2026 index values for A in Table 8.1.
K1 equals 0.14 x TC.
K2 is the CPP, EI, and QPIP federal credit calculated at the lowest federal rate. K3 is other federal non-refundable tax credits. K4 is the lesser of 0.14 x A, where A is annual gross income from office or employment before deductions, and 0.14 x CEA.

Step 3: annual federal tax payable T1 equals T3 - (P x LCF). If the result is negative, enter $0. For employees in Quebec, T1 equals [(T3 - (P x LCF)) - (0.165 x T3)], floored at $0.

Table 8.1 Rates (R), income thresholds (A), and constants (K) for 2026:
Federal A: 0, 58,523, 117,045, 181,440, 258,482.
Federal R: 0.1400, 0.2050, 0.2600, 0.2900, 0.3300.
Federal K: 0, 3,804, 10,241, 15,685, 26,024.

Table 8.2 Other rates and amounts for 2026:
Federal BPAF. Index rate 0.020. LCP rate 0.150. LCP amount 750. CEA 1,501 (1501).
QC abatement: 0.165.

Table 8.9 Federal claim codes using maximum BPAF:
Claim code 1, total claim amount from $0.00 to $16,452.00, Option 1 TC equals $16,452.00, Option 1 K1 equals $2,303.28.
