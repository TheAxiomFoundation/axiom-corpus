Title: CRA Schedule 7 2025 RRSP, PRPP, and SPP Contributions and Transfers, and HBP and LLP Activities.

Source:
- https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/5000-s7/5000-s7-25e.pdf

CRA Schedule 7 2025:
Part A calculates RRSP, PRPP, and SPP contributions. Line 4 adds contributions made from March 4, 2025 to December 31, 2025 and from January 1, 2026 to March 2, 2026. Line 5 adds unused contributions previously reported and available to deduct for 2025 to line 4.
Part B calculates HBP and LLP repayments. Line 9 adds HBP and LLP repayments. Line 10 subtracts line 9 from line 6 to determine contributions available to deduct.
Part C calculates the RRSP deduction. Line 13 subtracts employer PRPP contributions from the RRSP deduction limit. Line 16 subtracts transfers from contributions available to deduct. Line 17 is the lesser of line 13 and line 16. Line 18 is the contributions deducted for 2025, not more than line 17. Line 19 adds transfers and line 18. Line 20 is the lesser of line 10 and line 19 and is entered on line 20800.
Part D calculates unused contributions. Line 23 subtracts the RRSP deduction from contributions available to deduct.
Part E reports HBP withdrawals on line 24700 and LLP withdrawals on line 26300. Part F reports amateur athlete trust income on line 26700.
