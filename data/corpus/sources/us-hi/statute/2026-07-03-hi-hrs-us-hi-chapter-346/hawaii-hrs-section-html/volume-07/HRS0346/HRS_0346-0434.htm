[§346-434]  Annual report.  The office shall submit an annual report to the legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of each regular session that contains a summary of its activities during the preceding year, including:

     (1)  Actions taken to address issues, unmet needs, and challenges relating to wellness and resilience;

     (2)  Funds received pursuant to the activities of the office from federal, state, private, and philanthropic sources;

     (3)  The office's engagement with community entities and other stakeholders; and

     (4)  Any other findings and recommendations, including any proposed legislation. [L 2023, c 87, pt of §2]
