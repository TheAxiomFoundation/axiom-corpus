[Part XXI.]  OFFICE OF WELLNESS AND RESILIENCE

 

     [§346-431]  Definitions.  As used in this part, unless the context otherwise requires:

     "Board" means the wellness and resilience advisory board.

     "Office" means the office of wellness and resilience. [L 2023, c 87, pt of §2]
