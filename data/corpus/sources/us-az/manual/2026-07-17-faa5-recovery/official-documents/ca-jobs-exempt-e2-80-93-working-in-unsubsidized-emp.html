CA Jobs Exempt – Working in Unsubsidized Employment
 
REVISION 39
(12/01/15 –01/31/16)
A participant who is working in unsubsidized employment(g) is exempt from Jobs Program Participation when all of the following apply:
 
●Working an average of 30 hours or more per week
●Paid at least minimum wage
●Employment lasts at least 30 days
