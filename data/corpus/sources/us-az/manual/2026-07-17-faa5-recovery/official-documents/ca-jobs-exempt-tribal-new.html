FAA5.A Work Registration (WORW) : 03 Jobs Basic : C CA Jobs Exemptions : .02 CA Jobs Exempt - Tribal NEW
 
CA Jobs Exempt - Tribal NEW
 
REVISION 40
(02/01/16 – 03/31/16)
An enrolled tribal member who resides within the geographic area specified by a tribe as its Native Employment Work (NEW) Program area is exempt from Jobs participation. Key RT in the PAR/EXEM field on WORW.
