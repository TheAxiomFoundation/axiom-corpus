CA Jobs Work Program Requirements
 
REVISION 51
(01/01/23 – 12/31/23)
All of the following are required to participate in the Jobs Program unless exempt:
 
●All CA participants
●CA participants disqualified due to being a fleeing felon
●CA participants disqualified due to a felony drug conviction
 
(See CA Jobs mandatory referrals, JPPO FAA Requirements, and CA Jobs exemptions)
 
To locate the closest Jobs office to the participant, see Jobs Program Contact Information.
 
NOTE The contact information on the TANF Jobs website is for participant use only. FAA staff should not contact Jobs using any of those communication methods.
