CA Jobs Exempt – Age
 
REVISION 07
(01/01/09 – 03/31/09)
All dependent children under the age of 18 are exempt from Jobs Program participation.
 
Dependent children that are age 18, and meet CA Student criteria are also exempt from Jobs Program participation.
 
NOTE For a child that is age 18 and does not meet CA Student criteria, see Age 18 – Not Referred.
 
Key AG in the PAR/EXEM field on WORW.
 
EXCEPTION
A minor parent who is the head of household or the spouse(g) of the head of household is not exempt. Key RM in the PAR/EXEM field on WORW.
