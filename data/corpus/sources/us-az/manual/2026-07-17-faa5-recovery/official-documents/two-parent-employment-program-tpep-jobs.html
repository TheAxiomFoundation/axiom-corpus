Two Parent Employment Program (TPEP) Jobs
 
The Two Parent Employment Program (TPEP) is a pay-for-performance program. It serves two-parent budgetary units in which the primary wage earning parent (PWE) is unemployed or underemployed. TPEP services are time limited and are available state wide. To locate the closest Jobs office to the participant, see TPEP/Jobs Program Contact Information. Once the TPEP participants comply, the Jobs case manager releases the benefits.
