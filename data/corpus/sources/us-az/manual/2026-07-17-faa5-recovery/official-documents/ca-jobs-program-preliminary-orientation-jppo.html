CA Jobs Program Preliminary Orientation (JPPO)
 
REVISION 51
(01/01/23 - 12/31/23)
All new CA participants who are CA Jobs mandatory referrals are required to complete the Jobs Program Preliminary Orientation (JPPO) before CA approval.
 
Participants complete the JPPO compliance with an FAA worker using one of the following methods:
 
●Jobs Program Preliminary Orientation (JPPO) (FAA-1808A) Video
●Jobs Program Preliminary Orientation (JPPO) (FAA-1807A) Audio
●Jobs Program Preliminary Orientation (JPPO) (FAA-1806A) Script
 
The JPPO Compliance Video, Audio, or Script provides new CA participants information about the following:
 
●The Jobs Program
●Jobs Participation Requirements
●Jobs Work and Training Activities
●Jobs Supportive Services
 
The JPPO Compliance Video, Audio, or Script are located in the DES Document Center in English and Spanish.
 
The compliance requirement is met when the script titled “Jobs Program Preliminary Orientation (JPPO)” is read to the participant in English or their spoken language. To contact a translator directly, see Language Line Solutions. For more information on translation services, see A Translator is Needed under the Right To Request Translation Services. For participants requesting accommodations due to a visual impairment, large print version of the script is also available in the Document Center. For the hearing impaired, the script can be printed and provided to the participant.
 
The JPPO requirement does not apply to the following:
 
●Two Parent Employment Program (TPEP)
●Hopi Family assistance Program (HFAP)
●Salt River Pima-Maricopa Indian Community (SRPMIC) Life Enhancement and Resource Network (LEARN)
 
NOTE The Tribal TANF Programs have their own preapproval compliance requirement.
 
●Grant Diversion participants
●Participants that meet a CA Jobs Exemption
 
See JPPO FAA Procedures for instructions on completing the JPPO Pre-Compliance.
