FAA6.J Standards, Fees, and Amounts : 09 Utility Allowance Current Amount
 
Utility Allowance Current Amount
 
Previous Years
 
Effective 10/01/2025
 
The Utility Allowance current amounts are as follows:
 
●Standard Utility Allowance (SUA)
$323 for a budgetary unit with 1 to 3 participants
$438 for a budgetary unit with 4 or more participants
 
NOTE Do not count nonparticipants that are coded OU on SEPA
 
●Limited Utility Allowance (LUA)
$149 for a budgetary unit with 1 to 3 participants
$201 for a budgetary unit with 4 or more
 
NOTE Do not count nonparticipants that are coded OU on SEPA
 
●The Telephone Utility Allowance (TUA) current amount is $44
