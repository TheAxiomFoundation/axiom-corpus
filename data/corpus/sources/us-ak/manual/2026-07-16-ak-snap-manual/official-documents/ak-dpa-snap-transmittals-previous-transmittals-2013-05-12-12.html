Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2013-05

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Food Stamp Program

 

DATE:   December 2, 2013

 

This year Social Security announced a 1.5% Cost of Living Allowance (COLA) increase effective January 1, 2014. This manual change updates program policy in conjunction with the COLA increase, which affects the dollar fee limits charged for services performed as a representative payee. These fees are related to recipients of SSI and are subject to increase by the cost-of-living adjustment.

 

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

 

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at (907)465-3328 or email dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 602-3 B(2)  Unearned Income – SSI/SSA/VA Income

 

·         Updates COLA budget for SSI/SSA/VA income.
