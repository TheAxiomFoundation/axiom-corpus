STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2017-01

MANUAL:   Alaska SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

DATE:   April 3, 2017

 

We renamed this manual to reflect the federal name change from The Food Stamp Program to the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program with the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008. We have made changes to the self-employment annualization table based on changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines (FPGFederal Poverty Guidelines) for Alaska. We also updated the effective dates of Alaska's statewide waiver from the ABAWDAble-bodied Adult Without Dependents work requirements and time limits.  Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

 

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team via email at: dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 601- 4 A. VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS FOR INITIAL APPLICATIONS

 

      • Added information about secondary verification when eDRS shows an IPV.

      • Removed recertification from eDRS section of table a. Interfaces that must be checked

 

MS 602-1 G.(2)(i)  DETERMINING WORK REQUIREMENTS AND EXEMPTIONS

 

    • Removed language regarding TANF recipients being disqualified from SNAP when they are under a TANF work penalty

 

MS 602-1 G.(3)(a) DETERMINING WORK REQUIREMENTS AND EXEMPTIONS

 

      •  Added the Tribal TANF programs of: Maniilaq, BBNA  KANA  to the list of tribal organizations that provide TANF benefits and may impose penalties.

      •  Removed work requirement wording from the reason that TANF may be reduced.

 

MS 602-1 L.  ABLE-BODIED ADULTS WITHOUT DEPENDENTS

 

      •  Updated effective dates of ABAWD waiver

 

MS 602-2 B.  EXEMPT RESOURCES

 

      •  Added list of Retirement accounts that are excluded from resources by the Food and Nutrition Act of 2008.

      • Fixed ABLE expandable term

 

MS 602-2 E. TRANSFER OF RESOURCES

 

      •  Corrected the calculation in example in number #2.

 

MS 602-4 E.  DEDUCTIONS

 

      •  Updated with information for reviewing heating assistance awards (both State and Native Organizations) prior to making an eligibility determination.

      •  Add link to LIHEAPLow Income Heat and Energy Assistance Program award list on DPAweb.

 

MS 604-2 E.(2)(a) VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS: RECERTIFICATION

 

      • Removed recertification from eDRS section of table a. Interfaces that must be checked

 

MS 605-2 D. INCOME FROM SELF-EMPLOYMENT

 

      • Updated self-employment annualization standards with release of Federal Poverty Guidelines for Alaska, effective April 1, 2017.

 

ADDENDUM 2 ABAWD WORK REQUIREMENT EXEMPT & NON-EXEMPT   

 

      • Updated effective dates of the statewide ABAWD waiver.
