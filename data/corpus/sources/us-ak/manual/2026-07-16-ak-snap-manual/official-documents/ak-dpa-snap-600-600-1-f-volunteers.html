600-1 F.     VOLUNTEERS

 

Volunteers, or other persons not employed by DPADivision of Public Assistance, may be used in outreach activities, assisting in program promotion, teaching nutrition education, and providing transportation.  Volunteers may assist households in completing the application and forms and in obtaining required verification.  Volunteers may act as authorized representatives for households.  See MS 601-2F.

 

Except for fee agents, volunteers may not conduct interviews or certify an eligible applicant household.
