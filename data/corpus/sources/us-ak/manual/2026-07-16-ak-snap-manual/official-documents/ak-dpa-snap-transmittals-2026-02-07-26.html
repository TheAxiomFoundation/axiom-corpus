STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health

Division of Public Assistance

 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2026-02

 

MANUAL: Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

 

DATE: July 1, 2026

 

We have updated the minimum pend time frame given to households to provide requested information from 10 days to 15 days.

 

Highlights of this policy revision are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development team at hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 601-4      VERIFICATION

 

  • Replaced 10 day pend with 15 day pend for requested information.

 

MS 601-5      ACTIONS TAKEN ON THE APPLICATION

 

  • Replaced 10 day pend with 15 day pend for requested information.

 

MS 604-2      INTERIM REPORT

 

  • Revised example to use a 15 day pend.

 

MS 604-3      RECERTIFICATION

 

  • Replaced 10 day pend with 15 day pend for requested information.
  • Revised example to use a 15 day pend.

 

MS 604-4(A)      ACTING ON REPORTED CHANGES

 

  • Replaced 10 day pend with 15 day pend for requested information.

 

MS 604-4(C)      VERIFICATION REQUIREMENTS: CHANGES REPORTED DURING THE CERTIFICATION PERIOD

 

  • Revised example to use a 15 day pend.

 

MS 604-4(E)      ADDING A MEMBER TO THE HOUSEHOLD

 

  • Replaced 10 day pend with 15 day pend for requested information.

 

MS 605-3(B)      EXPEDITED SERVICE

 

  • Replaced 10 day pend with 15 day pend for requested information.
