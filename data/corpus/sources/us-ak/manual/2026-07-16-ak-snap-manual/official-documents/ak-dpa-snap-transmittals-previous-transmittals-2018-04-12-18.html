Division of Public Assistance 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2018-04

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

 

DATE:   December 3, 2018

 

This update added information regarding interfaces that can be used to verify residency. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

 

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development team at dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

    • Clarified which institutions allow SNAP recipients to receive benefits while residing in an institution that provides more than 50% of meals
