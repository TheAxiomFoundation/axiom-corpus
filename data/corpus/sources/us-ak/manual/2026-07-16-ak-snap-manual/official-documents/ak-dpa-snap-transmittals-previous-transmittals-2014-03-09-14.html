STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2014-03

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Food Stamp Program

 

DATE:   September 2, 2014

 

This update includes yearly changes to the income and utility standards that take effect October 1, 2014. In addition, the verification requirements to check IPV status and same-sex marriage definitions have both been updated to reflect current Federal Regulations.

 

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

 

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at (907)465-3328 or email dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 600-2  Definitions

  • Updates definition of spouse to include same-sex spouses.

 

MS 601-4  Verification  

  • Updates verification requirement to include IPV status check for new Alaska residents.

 

MS 602-1A Households

  • Updates households to reflect same-sex marriage recognition.

 

MS 602-1B  Residency

  • Updates verification requirement to include IPV status check for new Alaska residents.

 

MS 604-3G Adding a Member to the Household

  • Updates verification requirement to include IPV status check for new Alaska residents.

 

MS 604-4  Adverse Actions Requiring Only Adequate Notice

  • Clarifies adequate notice for certain households.

 

MS 605-1D  Institutions, Alcohol and Drug Addiction Treatment Centers

  • Updates contact information for approved treatment centers.

 

Addendum 4  Program Standards & Maximum Allotments

  • Updates standards and max shelter deductions.

  • Updates gross and net income limits.

  • Updates heating and non-heating utility standards.

 

Addendum 9  State Approved Substance Abuse Programs

  • Updates facilities list to reflect current approved programs.

  • Adds note that agencies may be umbrella agencies.
