Division of Public Assistance

 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2014-04

 

MANUAL: Alaska Food Stamp Program

 

DATE: October 1, 2014

 

The Farm Bill of 2008 (which governs Food Stamp rules) mandated the resource limits for the Food Stamp program increase each year based on the Consumer Price Index.  The limits for SPECAT households were increased to $3250 in 2011.  We received notice September 8, 2014, that the resource limits for other Food Stamp cases will increase to $2250 effective October 1, 2014.  There is no change to the resource limit for other programs.

 

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.


Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or e-mail dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES
