STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2017-03

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

 

DATE:   December 1, 2017

 

This update expanded and clarified information regarding identity verification and aligned the SNAP qualified alien policy with other manuals. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

 

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development team at dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 600-2         DEFINITIONS

 

    • Corrected link to Federal minimum wage.

 

MS 602-1 C.    IDENTITY

 

    • Expanded and clarified information regarding identity verification.

 

MS 602-1 D.    US CITIZENSHIP AND ELIGIBLE ALIEN STATUS

 

    • Aligned qualified alien policy and people exempt from “5-Year” waiting period with other manuals

 

MS 605-3B    EXPEDITED SERVICE

 

    • Clarified that postponed interviews are treated the same as other postponed verifications. If the client's interview is postponed and they "no-show" for the scheduled interview, they cannot have their interview postponed again.
