602-2          RESOURCES

 

Households must report all resources held at the time of application and any resources which the household anticipates receiving.

 

Maximum allowable values of nonexempt resources are:

 

  1. $3,000 per household; or

  2. $4,500 for Special Category (SPECATSpecial Category Household/Individual) households with at least one member disabled or age 60 or older.  See MS 600-2 for the definition of Special Category Household and Special Category Individual.
