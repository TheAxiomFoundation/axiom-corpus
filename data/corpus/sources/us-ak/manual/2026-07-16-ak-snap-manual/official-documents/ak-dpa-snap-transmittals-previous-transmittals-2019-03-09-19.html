TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2019-03

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

 

DATE:   September 16, 2019

 

This update includes policy revisions to ABAWDAble-bodied Adult Without Dependents related manual sections as Alaska implemented ABAWDAble-bodied Adult Without Dependents requirements as of October 1, 2019.

 

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development team at dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 600-2           DEFINITIONS      

 

    • Revised the definition of work to remove Work Fare as this option is not currently available in Alaska

 

MS 602-1(L)       ABLE-BODIED ADULTS WITHOUT DEPENDENTS (ABAWDs)

 

    • Revised due to ABAWDAble-bodied Adult Without Dependents requirements being implemented on October 1, 2019

 

Addendum 2       

 

    • Revised due to ABAWDAble-bodied Adult Without Dependents requirements being implemented on October 1, 2019
