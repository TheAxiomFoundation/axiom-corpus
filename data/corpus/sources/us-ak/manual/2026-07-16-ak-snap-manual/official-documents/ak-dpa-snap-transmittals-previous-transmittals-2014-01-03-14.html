STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2014-01

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Food Stamp Program

 

DATE:   March 3, 2014

 

We have made changes to the self-employment annualization table based on changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines (FPGFederal Poverty Guidelines) for Alaska. 

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

 

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at email: dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 600-1 G  Quality Assessment Reviews

 

  • Adds definitions and clarifications of policy for QAQuality Assessment refusal to cooperate and QAQuality Assessment failure to cooperate.

 

MS 602-1 I  Voluntary Job Quit and Reduction of Work Hours

 

  • Adds an example regarding job quit after application submission, but prior to interview.

 

MS 605-2 D (7)  Annualization Determination

 

  • Updates self-employment annualization standards with release of Federal Poverty Guidelines for Alaska, effective March 1, 2014.
