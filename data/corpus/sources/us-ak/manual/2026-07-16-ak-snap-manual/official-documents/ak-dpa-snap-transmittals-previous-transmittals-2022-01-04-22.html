STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2022-01

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

 

DATE:   April 1, 2022

 

We revised the self-employment annualization table based on changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines effective April 1, 2022.  Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field. 

 

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development team at hss.dpa.policy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 601-4(A)           Verification

 

  • Clarified the Prisoner Verification System (PVS) must be checked within IEVSInstant Eligibility Verification System at application and recertification.  
  • Clarified caseworkers are required to check eDRSElectronic Disqualified Recipient System at application and recertification, as well as when an adult household member is added to the case
  • Clarified INME 13 (SVESState Verification Exchange System) should be checked at application and recertification for death match. 

 

MS 602-4(E)       Deductions

 

  • Clarified for verification of heating costs when the person or agency the household is renting from charges for heat, the lease agreement or contact with the person or agency the household is renting from is an acceptable shelter cost verification. 

 

MS 602-4(F)       Deductions

 

  • Clarified a child support deduction is allowed only for children who are not living in the home. 

 

MS 604-2(E)       Recertification

 

  • Clarified the Prisoner Verification System (PVS) must be checked within IEVSInstant Eligibility Verification System at application and recertification.  
  • Clarified caseworkers are required to check eDRSElectronic Disqualified Recipient System at application and recertification, as well as when an adult household member is added to the case
  • Clarified INME 13 (SVESState Verification Exchange System) should be checked at application and recertification for death match. 

 

MS 604-3(c)       Reporting and Acting on Changes During the Certification Period

 

  • Added a bullet to clarify death match alerts received in EIS during the certification period should be worked. 

 

MS 604-4(D)       Notice Requirements During the Certification Period

 

  • Clarified notice is not required when benefits happen to increase due to a mass change.      

 

MS 605-2(D)       Specialized Budgeting

 

  • Clarified due to the nature of S-corporations in that income is passed through to the shareholder's pro-rata share, we need to obtain verification of the S-corporation tax forms verifying income that is said to have been received. 
  • Updated the Self-Employment Standards due to FPLFederal Poverty Level changes effective April 2022.
