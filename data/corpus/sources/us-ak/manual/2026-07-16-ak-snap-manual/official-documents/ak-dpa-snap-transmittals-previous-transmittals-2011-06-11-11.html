Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2011-06

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Food Stamp Program

 

DATE:   December 1, 2011

 

This year Social Security announced a 3.6% Cost of Living Allowance (COLA) increase effective January 2012.  This manual change updates program policy in conjunction with the COLACost of Living Allowance increase, which affect the dollar fee limits charged for services performed as a representative payee.  These fees are related to recipients of SSISupplemental Security Income and are subject to increase by the cost-of-living adjustment.

 

 If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or e-mail dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 602-3 B  UNEARNED INCOME

    • Updates the payee fee for SSISupplemental Security Income recipients due to the COLACost of Living Allowance increase effective January 2012.
