STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2012-03

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Food Stamp Program

 

DATE:   July 16, 2012

 

The Food Stamp program allows a deduction for medical expenses for individuals who are elderly or disabled.  The Food and Nutrition Service recently clarified that this medical deduction is not allowed for medical marijuana.  This manual change updates the Food Stamp manual to include medical marijuana as a medical expense that is not allowed.

 

If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or e-mail dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

602-4(D)(2) Medical Expenses not Allowed
  • Adds medical marijuana as an expense not allowed as a medical deduction for Food Stamps.
