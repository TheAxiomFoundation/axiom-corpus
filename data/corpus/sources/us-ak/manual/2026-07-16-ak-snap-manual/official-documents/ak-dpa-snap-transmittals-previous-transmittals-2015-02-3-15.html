            STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2015-02

 

MANUAL: Alaska Food Stamp Program

 

DATE: March 2, 2015

 

We have made changes to the self-employment annualization table based on changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines (FPGFederal Poverty Guidelines) for Alaska. In addition, we’ve updated the exception for battered shelter residents to include men.

 

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at email: dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 601-1 A Eligibility in One Food Stamp Household

  • Updated exception to include men as a resident of a shelter for battered persons.

 

MS 602-1 A Nonfinancial Eligibility - Households

  • Updated terminology to include men as a resident of a shelter for battered persons.

 

MS 604-3 G Adding a Member to the Household

  • Updated exception to include men as a resident of a shelter for battered persons.

 

MS 605-1 D Institutions

  • Updated terminology to include men as a resident of a shelter for battered persons.

 

MS 605-2 D (7) Annualization Determination

  • Updated self-employment annualization standards with release of Federal Poverty Guidelines for Alaska, effective March 1, 2015.
