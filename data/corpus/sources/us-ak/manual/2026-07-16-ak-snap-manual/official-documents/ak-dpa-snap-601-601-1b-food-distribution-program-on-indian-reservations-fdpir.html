601-1 B.     FOOD DISTRIBUTION PROGRAM ON INDIAN RESERVATIONS (FDPIRFood Distribution Program on Indian Reservations)

 

An individual or household cannot participate in SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program and Food Distribution Program on Indian Reservations (FDPIRFood Distribution Program on Indian Reservations) in the same month.  The caseworker will ensure SNAPSupplemental Nutrition Assistance Program benefits are not issued to participants of FDPIRFood Distribution Program on Indian Reservations.
