  STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2012-01

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Food Stamp Program

 

DATE:   March 1, 2012

 

We are making several changes to clarify existing policy as a result of suggestions from the field.  We have also made changes to the self-employment annualization table based on changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines (FPG) for Alaska. 

  Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at 465-3382 or e-mail dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 602-1 D  US Citizenship and Eligible Alien Status
  • Adds a link in subsection 4 to new subsection demonstrating when benefits begin when the 5 year waiting period ends.
  • Adds a new section to clarify when benefits begin at the end of the 5 year waiting period.
  MS 602-1 H  Caseworker Responsibilities – Work Requirements
  • Corrects notice number from X001 to F001.  The X001 does not exist.
  MS 602-1 I   Voluntary Job Quit and Reduction of Work Hours
  • Clarifies the process for job quit penalties for a semi-annual reporting household in subsection 2 e.
  • Adds examples to section 2 e.
  MS 604-3 G  Adding a Member To The Household
  • Clarifies that the same verification rules apply when adding new household members to an existing case that apply at application. 
  • Adds a link to the verification section in 601-4.
  MS 605-2 B  Excluded Members
  • Adds a note to subsection 1 a to clarify how to code TA income and shelter expenses in the household where the adult household members are excluded.
  MS 605-2 D  Income From Self-Employment
  • Updates the Annualization Chart based on an increase in federal poverty guidelines. 
  MS 605-2 E  Military Pay
  • Clarifies the amount of money used from a joint account by a deployed individual is not countable income to the family in subsection 1 a.
  • Adds SAVE pay to the list of excluded military income in subsection 2 b ii.
  • Clarifies that only the amount of military pay provided to the household is countable income in subsection 2 b ii.
  Addendum 3  Entitlements Table
  • Corrects SAVE pay to indicate it is not countable income.  This is considered combat pay.
  Addendum 7  Food Stamp Budget Worksheets
  • Updates links to current FSP 74 & 75 forms.
