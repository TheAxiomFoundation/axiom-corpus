STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER: 2016-04

 

MANUAL: Alaska Food Stamp Program

 

DATE:   October 3, 2016

 

This update includes yearly changes to the income and utility standards that take effect October 1, 2016.

 

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

 

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development Team via email at: dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 602-3(B)   Unearned Income

 

  • Added clarification of income budgeting for recoupments and mandatory payees from SSI/SSA/VA income.

 

MS 602-3(D)    Exempt Income

 

  • Updated Workforce Investment Act (WIA) to Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act of 2014 (WIOA).

 

MS 604-3(G)   Adding a member to the household

 

  • Clarified section regarding adding a new household member or child when reported the month before they join the household.

  • Removed an example that included information about reporting a new household member two months prior to their birth.

 

MS 605-1(B)   Students

 

  • Updated WIA Workforce Investment Act  to WIOA Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act of 2014.

  • Added mandatory training and on the job (OTJ) training as an exclusion to student status.

 

 

MS 605-2(E)    Military Pay

 

  •  Clarified and reorganized the countable military earned income section. Distinguished between countable earned income paid to families living off base and exempt meals provided on base to military personnel.

 

MS 605-3(B)    Expedited Services

 

  •  Added language to clarify that the waiver should not be applied if we’re out of time frames due to the 20% limitation established by FNS.

  • Clarified that SNAP benefits are issued on the fourth day in the example to ensure the field understands we use the waiver to stay within time frames.

 

 

Addendum 2   ABAWD work Requirement exempt and Non-Exempt Areas

 

  • Updated the “WADE HAMPTON CENSUS AREA” to “KUSILVAK CENSUS AREA”.

 

 

Addendum 4   Program Standards and Maximum Allotments

        

  • Updated the “WADE HAMPTON CENSUS AREA” to “KUSILVAK CENSUS AREA” in the Southwestern Utility Region.

  • Updated the FFY 17 maximum allotments and utility standards released by FNS.

 

Addendum 7   Budget Work Sheets

 

  • Updated the links to the FSP74 and FSP75 with updated standards information based on Addendum 4.

 

Addendum 8    Communities by Census Area

 

  • Updated the “WADE HAMPTON CENSUS AREA” to “KUSILVAK CENSUS AREA”.

 

Addendum 9    Approved Substance Abuse Programs

 

  • Updated the list of approved substance abuse treatment centers with Teen Challenge Pacific NW Adult and Teen Alaska CA due to their recent FNS approval.

  • Renamed Addendum 9 "Approved Substance Abuse Programs" and removed the word "State" from the title.
