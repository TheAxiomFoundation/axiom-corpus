STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2018-01

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program

 

DATE:   April 2, 2018

 

This update revised the self-employment annualization table based on changes in the Federal Poverty Guidelines ( FPG Federal Poverty Guidelines ) for Alaska and removed references to status reporting as there is no longer status reporting in Alaska effective April 1, 2018. Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

 

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below. If you have any questions, please contact the Policy & Program Development team at dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 601-3(A)       WHEN IS AN INTERVIEW REQUIRED

 

    • Added information that households meeting the elderly / disabled FNS waiver requirements may be exempt from the recertification interview requirement

    • Clarified that an interview does not need to be completed if a household submits their recertification after the end of the certification period but within the following month and is not due for their annual interview

 

MS 601-3(F)       CHANGES REPORTED DURING THE INTERVIEW

 

    • Removed references to status reporting households as there is no longer status reporting in Alaska effective April 1, 2018

 

MS 601-5(C)       PENDING THE APPLICATION

 

    • Clarified that an interview does not need to be completed if a household submits their recertification after the end of the certification period but within the following month and is not due for their annual interview

 

MS 602-1(A)       HOUSEHOLDS

 

    • Clarified household composition rules regarding homeless shelters

    • Added majority custody language to manual due to a fair hearing loss

    • Added clarification that proof of compliance with drug felon criteria is required

    • Added explanation of the four-part test to establish fleeing felon status

 

MS 602-1(I)        VOLUNTARY JOB QUIT AND REDUCTION OF WORK HOURS

 

    • Removed references to status reporting households as there is no longer status reporting in Alaska effective April 1, 2018

 

MS 602-2(A)       DEFINITION AND TYPES OF RESOURCES

 

    • Added clarification regarding crowdfunding accounts

 

MS 604-1(A)       LENGTH OF CERTIFICATION PERIOD

 

    • Removed references to status reporting households as there is no longer status reporting in Alaska effective April 1, 2018

    • Clarified circumstances in which shorter certification periods can be provided

 

MS 604-2            RECERTIFICATION

 

    • Added information regarding the new FNS waiver allowing Alaska to waive the recertification interview for certain elderly / disabled households

    • Clarified that an interview does not need to be completed if a household submits their recertification after the end of the certification period but within the following month and is not due for their annual interview


MS 604-3            REPORTING AND ACTING ON CHANGES DURING THE CERTIFICATION PERIOD

 

    • Removed references to status reporting households as  there is no longer status reporting in Alaska effective April 1, 2018

 

MS 604-3(G)       PUBLIC ASSISTANCE INCOME CHANGES

 

    • Reworded section to clarify that SNAP overpayments are worked with the amount of TANF that should have been issued unless fraud is involved

 

MS 605-1(D)      INSTITUTIONS

 

    • Updated section DAC/GLA rules to match FCEA 2008 and new reporting requirements at 7 CFR 273.11(e) and 7 CFR 273.11(f)

 

MS 605-2(D)7    ANNUALIZATION DETERMINATION

 

    • Updated self-employment annualization standards with release of the Federal Poverty Guidelines for Alaska, effective April 1, 2018

 

MS 605-3(B)       EXPEDITED SERVICE

 

    • Clarified information regarding the postponed interview waiver

    • Added examples illustrating when and when not to use the waiver outside of the expedite timeframes

 

MS 605-7(I)          PFD REPORTING REQUIREMENTS

 

    • Removed references to status reporting households as there is no longer status reporting in Alaska effective April 1, 2018
