STATE OF ALASKA

Department of Health and Social Services

Division of Public Assistance

 

 

TRANSMITTAL NUMBER:   2014-02

 

MANUAL:   Alaska Food Stamp Program

 

DATE:   June 2, 2014

 

We have added examples to the exceptions to household definitions for children under the age of 18 that are homeless. In addition, we have also added an example to better clarify when an APA household is considered a simplified reporting household.

 

Other changes are either technical in nature or clarifications that are the results of policy questions or suggestions from the field.

 

Highlights of these and other policy revisions are described below.  If you have any questions, please contact the Policy and Program Development Team at email: dpapolicy@alaska.gov.

 

OVERVIEW OF CHANGES

 

MS 600-2  Definitions

  • Updates the definition of a child.

 
MS 602-1A (2)(c)  Exceptions to Household Definitions

  • Adds an example clarifying when a homeless child does not need to include a family they are temporarily residing with.
  • Adds an example clarifying when a homeless child does need to include a family they are residing with.

  
MS 604-3B  Status Reporting

  • Adds an example clarifying when an APAAdult Public Assistance household is not a status reporting household.
