Canada child benefit (CCB), payment period July 2026 to June 2027, based on 2025 adjusted family net income (AFNI):
AFNI is family net income from line 23600, minus UCCB and RDSP income from lines 11700 and 12500, plus UCCB and RDSP repayments from lines 21300 and 23200.
Maximum CCB for each eligible child under 6 is 8157 per year. Maximum CCB for each eligible child aged 6 to 17 is 6883 per year.
If AFNI is under 38237, there is no reduction. If AFNI is greater than 38237 up to 82847, reduce by 7 percent for one child, 13.5 percent for two children, 19 percent for three children, and 23 percent for four or more children, of income greater than 38237.
If AFNI is greater than 82847, reduce by 3123 plus 3.2 percent of income greater than 82847 for one child, 6022 plus 5.7 percent for two children, 8476 plus 8 percent for three children, and 10260 plus 9.5 percent for four or more children.
Shared custody pays each parent 50 percent of what they would have received if they had full custody, calculated using their own AFNI.

Child Disability Benefit (CDB), payment period July 2026 to June 2027, based on 2025 AFNI:
Maximum CDB is 3480 per year, 290 per month, for each child eligible for the disability tax credit.
The CDB starts being reduced when AFNI is greater than 82847. The reduction is 3.2 percent of AFNI greater than 82847 for families with one eligible child. The reduction is 5.7 percent of AFNI greater than 82847 for families with two or more eligible children.

Canada Groceries and Essentials Benefit (CGEB), payment period July 2026 to June 2027, based on 2025 AFNI:
For married or common-law families, under 46432 AFNI the annual amount is 890 with no children, 1124 with 1 child, 1358 with 2 children, 1592 with 3 children, and 1826 with 4 children or more. The child increment in this table is 234 for each child up to the four-children-or-more row.
For single individuals and single parents, under 11564 AFNI the annual no-child amount is 445. The single no-child amount rises by 2 percent of AFNI over 11564 until it reaches 679. Single parents with children use the same child table amounts: 1124 with 1 child, 1358 with 2 children, 1592 with 3 children, and 1826 with 4 children or more.
The CGEB table shows a 5 percent reduction of the annual amount for AFNI greater than 46432. Example table values include married/common-law with 2 children at AFNI 60000 receiving 679.60, and single no-child at AFNI 50000 receiving 500.60.
The yearly entitlement is paid in 4 quarterly issuances every three months in July 2026, October 2026, January 2027, and April 2027. If the amount per quarter is less than 50, the entire amount is paid as a single payment in July.

Canada Carbon Rebate (CCR) for individuals:
The Canada Carbon Rebate for individuals, formerly the Climate action incentive payment, was closed. On March 15, 2025, the Government of Canada stopped the federal fuel charge and the Canada Carbon Rebate for individuals. There are no further quarterly CCR payments after the April 2025 payment.
