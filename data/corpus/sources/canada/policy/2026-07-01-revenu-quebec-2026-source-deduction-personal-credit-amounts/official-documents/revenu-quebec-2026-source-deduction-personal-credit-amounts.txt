Title: Revenu Québec 2026 source deduction personal credit amounts.

Source:
- https://www.revenuquebec.ca/en/businesses/source-deductions-and-employer-contributions/employers-principal-changes-for-2026/

Revenu Québec principal changes for 2026:
The personal income tax system is indexed at 2.05% for 2026.
The personal tax credit amounts on the Source Deductions Return TP-1015.3-V are indexed for 2026.
Basic personal amount: 18952.
Amount transferred from one spouse to the other: 18952.
Amount for other dependants: 5684.
Amount for a child under 18 enrolled in post-secondary studies, per term: 3901.
Additional amount for a person living alone in a single-parent family: 2681.
Amount for a severe and prolonged impairment in mental or physical functions: 4208.
Amount for a person living alone: 2172.
Age amount: 3986.
Amount for retirement income: 3541.
Reduction threshold for the age amount, the amount for a person living alone and the amount for retirement income: 42955.
Exclusion threshold for eligible work income for calculating the amount for career extension: 7655.
Maximum amount of eligible work income for calculating the amount for career extension: 12755.
Reduction threshold for the amount for career extension: 57660.
The threshold for determining the method to calculate source deduction of income tax on gratuities and retroactive pay is 18952 for 2026.
The maximum deduction for workers is 1450 for 2026.
The maximum exemption for an emergency services volunteer is 1450 for 2026.
For 2026, income tax must be withheld at a rate of 14% from the taxable portion of a registered disability savings plan disability assistance payment that exceeds 23160.
