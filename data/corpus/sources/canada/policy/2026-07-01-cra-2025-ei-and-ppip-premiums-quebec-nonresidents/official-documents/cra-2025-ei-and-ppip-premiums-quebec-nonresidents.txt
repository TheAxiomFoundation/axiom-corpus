CRA Schedule 10 2025 Employment Insurance and Provincial Parental Insurance Plan Premiums

Source:
- https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/5005-s10/5005-s10-25e.pdf
- https://www.canada.ca/content/dam/cra-arc/formspubs/pbg/5005-s10/5005-s10-25e.txt

Decimal rates used by the encoded formulas are 0.00878, 0.43736, 0.00494, and 0.0131.
Normalized numeric values used by parameters include 98000, 860.44, 376.32, 484.12, 2000, 65700, and 860.67.

Part A - PPIP premiums payable on self-employment income:
Line 1 uses net business income from line 27 of Schedule L of the Revenu Quebec income tax return.
Line 2 is maximum PPIP insurable income, $98,000.00.
Line 3 uses employment income from box 14 of all T4 slips, including employment income from outside Canada and employment income without a T4 slip, or box 56 if applicable.
Line 4 is line 2 minus line 3, floored at zero.
Line 5 is the lesser of line 1 and line 4.
Line 6 is the PPIP rate on self-employment income, 0.878%, encoded as decimal 0.00878.
Line 7 is line 5 multiplied by line 6, maximum $860.44.
Line 8 is the applicable rate, 43.736%, encoded as decimal 0.43736.
Line 9 multiplies line 7 by line 8 and is entered on line 22300, maximum $376.32.
Line 10 is line 7 minus line 9 and is entered on line 31215, maximum $484.12.

Part B - PPIP premiums payable on employment income:
Line 11 uses employment income from T4 slips where province of employment is outside Quebec, including outside-Canada employment income, exempt outside-Quebec income, and employment income without a T4 slip.
Line 12 is maximum PPIP insurable income, $98,000.00.
Line 13 uses PPIP insurable earnings from Quebec T4 slips box 56, or box 14 if box 56 is blank.
Line 14 is line 12 minus line 13, floored at zero.
Line 15 is the lesser of line 11 and line 14.
Line 16 is the PPIP rate on employment income, 0.494%, encoded as decimal 0.00494.
Line 17 multiplies line 15 by line 16 and is entered on line 31210, maximum $484.12.

Part C - Employment insurance overpayment:
Line 18 uses total EI insurable earnings from T4 box 24, or box 14 if box 24 is blank unless box 28 shows EI-exempt earnings, includes employment income without a T4 slip, enters zero if total earnings are $2,000 or less, and has maximum $65,700.
Line 19 is the EI rate on self-employment and other earnings, 1.31%, encoded as decimal 0.0131.
Line 20 is line 18 multiplied by line 19, maximum $860.67.
Line 21 is total EI premiums from T4 box 18 plus EI premiums from pay stubs for employment income without a T4 slip.
Line 22 is the amount from line 20.
Line 23 is line 21 minus line 22, floored at zero, and is entered on line 45000.
Line 24 is the lesser of line 20 and line 21 and is entered on line 31200.
