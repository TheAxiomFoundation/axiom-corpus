Calculating Benefit Level

To determine the household’s benefit level, subtract 30% of the net adjusted income from the Thrifty Food Plan for the appropriate household size.

Minimum Benefit

BBCE Households

 

One and Two Person Households-BBCE

  • Eligible one and two person households are eligible for a monthly minimum benefit. Refer to Appendix B – Table of Standards.

    Exception: In the initial month, proration is applied to the minimum benefit. If during the initial month the benefit is less than $10 due to proration, the household is eligible for a zero benefit.

     

Households with Three or More Members - BBCE

  • Eligible households with three or more members may be eligible for a benefit of less than the monthly minimum benefit.

     

    Exception: If during the initial month due to proration the benefit is less than $10, a zero benefit is authorized.

     

Non-BBCE Households

Non-BBCE household will follow original benefit determinations, which include the gross and net income test.

 

Exception: If during the initial month due to proration the benefit is less than $10, a zero benefit is authorized.
