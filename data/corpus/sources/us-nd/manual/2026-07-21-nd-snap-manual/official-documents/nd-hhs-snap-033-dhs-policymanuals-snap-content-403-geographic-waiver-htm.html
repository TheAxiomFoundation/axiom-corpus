SNAP:

Able-Bodied Adults Without Dependents Able-bodied adults between the ages of 18-54 (subject to change) without dependents (ABAWDs) are eligible for participation in SNAP for only three (3) months within a 36-month period of time without working or participating in a work or welfare program for at least 20 hours weekly, averaged monthly, unless exempt. After exhausting the initial three (3) allowable months, ABAWD individuals meeting certain criteria may qualify for three (3) additional months of participation in the 36-month period. (ABAWD)

403 Geographic Waiver

Overview

At the request of the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), the Secretary of Agriculture may waive the Able- Bodied Adults Without Dependents (ABAWD) provision for an identified group of individuals.
