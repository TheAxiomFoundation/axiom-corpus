1004 Adding/Removing Household Member

Overview

When an individual moves into or out of a SNAP household People who buy and prepare food together. Spouses, parents, and children who live together are usually counted as the same household., eligibility and the benefit amount must be evaluated based on the new household composition and the household's reporting requirements. The eligibility staff member must use the correct time frames when it is necessary to provide the household with a notice of adverse action.

 

Adding a Household Member

The eligibility staff member acts on changes in household composition within 10 days of the reported/discovered change Information that is different from what is currently used to determine eligibility and/or benefits.. When the household provides necessary information/verification Third-party information or documentation used to establish the accuracy of statements. within 10 days of the date of information request notice, the new household member is added effective the month after the reported/discovered change.

 

A new household member is added to the SNAP household effective the month after the reported/discovered change when the household provides any necessary information/verification to add the new member within 10 days from the date of request for information notice. When the 10th day of the request period falls on a weekend or holiday, the household has until the next business day The official working days of the week defined as the days between and including Monday to Friday, but do not include weekends and those public holidays during which the North Dakota Department of Health and Human Services is closed. If an application or review is filed, a change is reported or verification is provided on a day in which the human service zone office is closed but the Department is open, it will be considered received on that date. to provide the information/verification.

 

If a child or children are reported as new member(s) and they are already receiving SNAP on another case, the household composition of the reporting household is questionable and must be verified. The new household must verify conclusively that the child(ren) is in their household and not in the household where they are currently receiving benefits. Children cannot be removed from their current case unless both households agree to the changed living arrangement OR we have compelling verification from a third party, for example, Child and Family Services, a court order, or result of a fraud investigation. If the child(ren) is living in more than one household and the households share the child equally, the households must decide whose case the child will receive benefits on. Special consideration is given in cases where an adult has left a household with children due to domestic violence and is living in a shelter.

 

TANF and SNAP Supplement

If adding the new member results in TANF and SNAP supplements, The TANF supplement Additional benefits issued for the current month to compensate for an under issuance. issued is countable income The total of earned and unearned income not excluded by policy, expected to be received by the household for the budget/benefit month..

 

Removing an Household Member

The eligibility staff member acts on changes in household composition within 10 days of the reported/discovered change. A household member is removed from the household effective the month after the reported/discovered change.
