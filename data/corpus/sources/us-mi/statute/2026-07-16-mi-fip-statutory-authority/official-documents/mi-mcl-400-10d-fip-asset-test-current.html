THE SOCIAL WELFARE ACT (EXCERPT)


Act 280 of 1939


400.10d Determination of financial eligibility for family independence program; application of asset test.

Sec. 10d.

    For the purposes of determining financial eligibility for the family independence program administered under this act, the department must apply an asset test.

    

    

History: Add. 2012, Act 79, Imd. Eff. Apr. 11, 2012 ;-- Am. 2023, Act 53, Eff. Feb. 13, 2024
